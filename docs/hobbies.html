When I wasn't doing work for my research internship at CMU, I'd often explore the streets of Pittsburgh. I found myself enjoying photography, and it's been something I love to do since!

Finding my parent's old digital camera (Olympus D560 Zoom for those curious) has been a game changer. Before, I just used my iPhone, but there's something about using a specialized device to take photos.

Drawing was one of my favorite things as a kid, and something I've gotten back into the past few years while searching for new hobbies. My drawings back then were always chaotic and maximalist, simply because I enjoyed the freedom of drawing whatever came to my mind that I thought looked cool. I try to capture that same childhood wonder, but mixed with my newfound intrigue for typography and graffiti.
